All-Party Parliamentary Group for the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus.

The All-Party Parliamentary Group (APPG) for the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus (TRNC) was created with the purpose to promote the interest of the people of Northern Cyprus and plays a cruical role in developing positive relations between the United Kingdom and the TRNC.

The current members of the APPG for the TRNC are:

Chair: Yasmin Qureshi

Co-Chair: Lord Northbrook

Co-vice chair: Baroness Hussein-Ece

Co-vice chair: Lord Sharkey

Co-vice chair: Graham Stringer

Co-vice chair: Lord Harrison
